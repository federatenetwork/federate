Federate Network

A human web, built by people.

No feeds. No scraping. No AI training by default.

Manifesto

Federate Network is a human web protocol. It is not an AI-training platform, an ad network, an engagement-farming social feed, a surveillance platform, or a content scraping machine.

It exists to make the web human again: human authorship, personal websites, small communities, creative spaces, public knowledge, ownership, portability, participant-operated infrastructure, content preservation, local-first identity — a slower, more intentional web.

Your content is not used for AI training by default. The network is designed around people creating spaces, not platforms extracting attention.

The namespaces

  • .fed official Federate namespace — specs, registry, status, governance
  • .pagina personal sites, blogs, portfolios, essays
  • .rosa creative, visual, poetic, art-oriented spaces
  • .cara identity, profiles, people pages, public cards
  • .mosca weird internet — experiments, memes, small games, underground pages
  • .tipos / .types typography, type design, lettering, fonts

Federate controls the root; TLDs can be delegated to operators who issue domains inside them. Every record is signed — root key, TLD operator key, domain owner key — down to hash-verified content blocks.
